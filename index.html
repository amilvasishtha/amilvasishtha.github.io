About Me

Hello! Welcome!

My name's Amil and I am an aspiring full-stack web developer.

I have recently completed an 8-week training course in May of this year (2015) with Founders and Coders.

I am currently working with Founders and Coders on Minimum Viable Products and other projects, building on my current set of skills as well as picking up some new ones!

I have used Bootstrap to create this site, no point reinventing the wheel right? It uses full HTML, CSS, and custom JavaScript files, along with LESS files for easy customization.

Profile

So I'm 24 years old. I graduated from Queen Mary, University of London in 2012 with a BSc in Mathematics.

Post graduating I had some work experience at Standard Chartered Bank. I then went on to become an analyst at Practico, a small firm of cost lawyers.

After about 2 years I felt I needed a change. Something different. Something that allowed me to exercise both my creativite and mathemtical skills.

I had always been interested in web development but didn't know a huge amount about it. I had been briefly exposed to programming during a mathematical computing course in my first year of university, but that was about it.

After searching the net I came across Founders and Coders. I checked out their website and decided to apply. I was picked from a collection of talented, aspiring developers to undergo an 8-week course. The rest is history.

I am now on my way to becoming a full-stack developer!
